Founder's note

Building practical learning paths for modern careers

As the Founder and Chairman of TK INFO-TECH, I am committed to helping students and working professionals build the skills needed for today's fast-evolving IT landscape. My background in computer applications, machine learning, and UI/UX design shaped a learning environment that values both strong fundamentals and real implementation.

The goal of TK INFO-TECH is simple: reduce the gap between what industry expects and what learners confidently deliver. That means practical training, project experience, mentorship, and a learning experience that feels supportive instead of overwhelming.

Practical learning

Courses focus on applying skills through projects, guided tasks, and real examples.

Career readiness

We help learners build resumes, confidence, and better preparation for interviews and internships.

Supportive mentoring

Questions, feedback, and one-to-one guidance are part of the experience, not an afterthought.

What we stand for

Technology education and career services that feel clear, relevant, and encouraging

We aim to create a space where learners can build strong technical foundations, explore current tools, and move toward meaningful opportunities with more confidence through both education and service support.
